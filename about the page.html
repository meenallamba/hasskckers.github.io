Women are a highly underrated group in the STEM field. With this said, Her Code(an exclusive hackathon for girls organized by Neerja Modi School) is supporting and celebrating women innovators and inviting women developers, creators, designers, and hackathon enthusiasts to showcase their projects to the world and take them to the next level.

The is a blog created in this event by Meenal Lamba, Alankrita Rana, Avni Rinwa and we Hasskckers! support this intiative by our school and hence, created this website.
